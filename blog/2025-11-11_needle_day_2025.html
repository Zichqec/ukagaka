Needle v1.1.5

Needle has been updated to v1.1.5! We added relatively few dialogues this year, but, well... we couldn't just skip over the 1111 milestone! A few other little features and easter eggs have been added as well.

Changelog:

  • Added 111 new dialogues, bringing the total to 1111.

  • Added a dressup randomizer. This can be activated by starting a mouse gesture and doing 5 circles in either the clockwise or counterclockwise direction (without doubling back).

  • Added a rare boot dialogue.

  • Spectre reactions, booting, and closing, all now get uwu text when using the Blushie shell with the uwu mode activated.

  • General formatting/typo fixes.

  • Updated to YAYA Tc571-13.
