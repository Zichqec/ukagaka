This is a mirror download for the music player ghost FLELE. It was originally hosted at navy.nm.land.to/.

Update 2026-04-02: land.to has shut down, and as a result the original website linked above no longer works. (My mirror is unaffected.) The link is left here for viewing through the Internet Archive or other similar services.

Another mirror of FLELE has been created on GitHub by Ponapalt, and has been updated to YAYA and set to UTF-8. Ukadon post with the relevant details.

The rest of this page was originally written at a time when the Internet Archive was temporarily unavailable, and some users could not access the original FLELE site. It is left intact for posterity.

For many English-speaking users, the original site is inaccessible, giving a 403 error. You can often get around this by using the Internet Wayback Machine, however, this service is currently down due to the Internet Archive being hacked.

Thankfully, the original website says that redistribution of FLELE is permitted, so I have created a mirror here on my site. Use it if the original site does not work for you for whatever reason.

This ghost is not mine and I do not lay claim to it in any way, it is listed here only for archival purposes/to ensure all users have access to it.

You may also be interested in FLELE's page on the Ukagaka Dream Team Wiki, which has more information about the ghost and how to use it.

There is also an English translation created by Zarla.
