Free Assets

The following are free assets that I've made for the purposes of creating ukagaka. They are not intrinsically linked to ukagaka, but can still be used for them in various ways.

Permissions may vary depending on what asset you're looking at, so please check any included information (such as a readme file) before using these assets. In general:

  • You can use these assets for anything that is within the realm of ukagaka (ghosts, shells, balloons, calendar skins, owner draw menus, etc.). Please do not use them for things that are not ukagaka related.
  • You can modify the assets to suit your project (changing colors, hue shifting, cropping, transforming, etc.), but do not turn them into anything discriminatory or political.
  • Do not redistribute these assets on their own.
  • Do not use these assets for commercial works.
  • Credit may or may not be required, depending on the asset. If it's required or you just want to give credit regardless, I can be credited as Zichqec, with the following link: https://ukagaka.zichqec.com/

If the permissions listed in the readme/information for a particular asset do not match the above, that individual asset's permissions take precedence.
