About

A tattooed girl with a lollipop, in a tanktop! Her leg moves back and forth while she idles, occasionally kicking forward.

She comes with the standard expressions, plus variations for eyes pointing different directions, a couple of arm poses, and some special expressions. There is also a blush and a sweat drop that can be applied to any surface with \i[] tags.
